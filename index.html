Frequently asked questions

Athletes spend a lot of time training and traveling for competitions. This is on top of family and school commitments, which makes it difficult for most athletes to keep a consistent full-time job. We created this platform to launch a project to help amateur athletes make money. This is an ideal time given the changes to NIL rules at the NCAA level but this is relevant to athletes across high school, college, Olympics, and professional sports.
A majority of aspiring professional, college, or Olympic athletes pay out of pocket for their training, including travel, tournament costs, housing, food and supplements, personal trainers, and physical therapy. Depending on the sport and level these costs come to. We allow athletes to create their own NFTs using their videos of their special moments, or tricks they want to share with fans. The money earned goes to the athletes.
Each card is unique, and members will receive air drops of digital and physical goods. We will also allow members early access to select cards.
We have plans for gamification of these cards. We do not want to say too much at this time but can say we hope to make it fun for fans and collectors.
